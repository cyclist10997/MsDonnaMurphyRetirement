Visitor Access Information

Guests who do not have a valid Department of Defense ID should plan for additional time when arriving at West Point. The Visitor Control Center is located at 2107 New South Post Road, Highland Falls, NY 10928 and is open daily from 6:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m..

West Point is a REAL ID compliant installation. Visitors age 18 and older should bring a valid photo ID. A current driver’s license can be accepted if you have a social security card or birth certificate. Current passports are also accepted in place of a REAL ID.

Visitors arriving with a qualified escort who holds a DoD ID do not need to stop at the Visitor Control Center, but they must remain with that escort while on the installation.

For the most current access requirements and additional details, please visit West Point Visitor Information . You may also contact the Visitor Control Center at (315) 774-9235 or (315) 774-9234.
